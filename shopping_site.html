FOR ALL YOUR CLIMBING NEEDS

Five-Ten - Niad Lace

Five-Ten's new version of the widely loved Anasazi Pinks, the Niad Lace is an all-round climbing shoe that feels right at home, whether you are on Granite or Sandstone, Face or Crack, or even honing your craft indoors. The Niad Lace gives the platform to feel solid through the crux of your recent project.

La Sportiva - Skwama

The La Sportiva Skwama is a high end, mid-stiffness performance shoe which used the P3 midsole technology to keep the aggressive profile of the shoe over its lifetime. Some exciting new features of the shoe include the S-Heel construction which uses two different types of rubber compound to maintain rigidity and stability in the heel no matter which direction your heel hooks are pulling in. There is also a split sole which allows the sole to spread a little when weighted, this reduces any edge deformation.

Black Diamond - Method

Built to make quick work of sheer pitches of steep or vertical climbing, the Black Diamond Method provides sublime forefoot comfort thanks to its minimized break-in period and offers a stiff edge, for ultimate control and increased power on varied terrain.

Beal - Joker Soft 9.1mm UNICORE Dry Cover

The Beal Joker Soft 9.1mm UNICORE Dry Cover is identical to the regular Joker bar a slightly different construction process that makes it suppler and smoother. As such it's ideal for climbers after maximum performance. With a triple rating and sheath treatment, the Beal Joker Soft 9.1mm UNICORE Dry Cover is ideal for sport and trad climbers looking for a rope that can do a bit of everything.

Black Diamond - 8.9 Dry Climbing Rope

The Black Diamond 8.9 Rope Dry is a triple-rated rope with a dry treatment. Being rated as single, half, and twin, the 8.9 can be used as a performance single rope or as a fatter half or twin rope that can be paired with a skinnier model, making it a versatile rope for the climber who wants something they can use for sport one day and alpine the next.

PETZL - GriGri

The Petzl GriGri is the latest redesign of the classic assisted-braking device. The GriGri lets climbers get on with climbing without worrying about the belayers actions interfering with progression up a route through a smoother belaying action, even if the actual belay technique remains the same. The Petzl GriGri now allows the use of thinner ropes, you can now use diameters of between 8.5 and 11mm. Even though thinnner ropes are now facilitated, the lowering of a climber is actually smoother due to a revised descent control system giving a larger 'sweet spot' and more progressive assisted braking capability. The icing on the cake is that the GriGri only weighs in at 175g, is 5% lighter, and with an updated design is more durable and easy-to-use than the GriGri 2.

DMM - Dragon Cam- Size 2

The DMM Dragon Cam - Size 2 has an expansion range of 22-41mm. Its lobes have been dubbed 'TripleGrip' which have a raw aluminium contact area and additional bite points, this acts to increase friction and holding power especially in soft rock or sub-optimal placements.

Black Diamond - Capitan Helmet

Engineered and intelligently designed to handle time on the most epic walls the world has to offer, the Black Diamond Capitan Helmet is an ultra-durable workhorse, that features added coverage on the sides and back, and a cradle fit for all-day comfort. Boasting a 2-piece ABS shell construction that allows for an optimized fit, the Black Diamond Capitan makes use of a fusion of EPP foam and an EPS foam puck, which meets the proposed UIAA increased side and back protection requirements. Providing ample breathability with its ventilation system and a low profile suspension system to get the right fit, the Capitan Helmet has all of the comfort, technical specifications, and features of a top range helmet, but without the hefty price tag.

Edelrid - Salathe

When you are trying to keep weight to a minimum but do not want to compromise on safety the Edelrid Salathe is an excellent choice of helmet. Its ultra light and well ventilated design utilises a combination of a foam injected EPP core with a partial ABS hard shell.
